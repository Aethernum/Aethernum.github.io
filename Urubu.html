What is URUBU ?

Join the adventure in the cooperative action game "URUBU". Developed by a talented team of 8 people in 10 weeks as part of the Ubisoft project.
This game offers an exciting and thrilling gaming experience for players.
With stunning graphics and smooth gameplay, "URUBU" immerses players in a vibrant world filled with action and adventure. Play cooperatively with friends to defeat enemies, explore incredible environments and complete challenging missions.

The game was awarded the Special Jury Prize, a testament to the exceptional quality of "URUBU".
