What is MoonStone Kingdom ?

Dive into the medieval world of "Moonstone Kingdom", a unique role-playing game that combines dungeon management and exploration.
Play as a knight who must make his kingdom prosper by carrying out the King's missions such as repairing buildings or attacking goblin-infested dungeons to collect treasures and resources.
Between the challenges of managing your kingdom and the epic battles in the dungeons, "Moonstone Kingdom" offers you an exciting and varied gameplay experience.
