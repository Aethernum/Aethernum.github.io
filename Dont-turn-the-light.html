What is Don't Turn the Light OFF?

Escape from an alien ship in this 2D top down game. In this exciting game, you play as a robot who must escape from an alien ship by carefully managing its light source. With simple yet eye-catching graphics and intuitive gameplay, "Don't Turn the Light OFF" offers a stimulating challenge for players of all skill levels.

Explore the labyrinthine corridors of the alien ship and use your light sparingly to avoid running out of power (you may not be alone in the dark corridors).
