What is Blow the bug ?

Join the excitement of "Blow the bug", a two-player arcade game inspired by a popular Japanese game show. Compete against a friend or an opponent in real time to determine who will be the champion of this fast-paced game.
With fast-paced gameplay, "Blow the bug" offers a thrilling challenge for all skill levels. Use your speed and agility to beat your opponent in fast-paced rounds.
